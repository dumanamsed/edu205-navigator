The whole course builds one school.

Fifteen weeks, five projects, a podcast, a magazine. It looks like a pile. It is not. Every essential question in this course is a design decision in disguise, and every project you turn in becomes a piece of the final exam: the public school your group reimagines for a real U.S. community and presents as a conference poster in December. This is not a syllabus. It is the course taken apart so you can see how it actually works. Click anything.
